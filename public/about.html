A flashcard tool made for students, by students.

What is ?

is a flashcard-based web app designed to help students learn Japanese efficiently. As a student at MCC taking Japanese 101, I wanted a tool dedicated to mastering vocabulary in an engaging and effective way.

Key Features

  • Custom Flashcards: Create and manage your own personalized flashcards.
  • Practice & Test Modes: Learn at your own pace or challenge yourself with timed recall.
  • Offline Storage: Flashcards are saved in your browser, no server required.
  • Share & Import Sets: Generate codes to share your sets with others.
